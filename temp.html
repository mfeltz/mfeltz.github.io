About Me

My name is Margaret Feltz and I am junior at Tufts University studying Computer Science Engineering with a minor in Entrepreneurial Science. While I enjoy doing some web development in my spare time, I also have many other passions that shape who I am. On my campus, I serve the role of president for Tufts China Care, a non-profit that fundraises to provide life-changing surgeries for orphans in Beijing. I also am a co-director of the LUX Fashion Show on my campus, which is China Care's largest funraiser, but also a very large scale production. I also enjoy being one of the executives of Tufts' student run business Tufts Student Resources.
